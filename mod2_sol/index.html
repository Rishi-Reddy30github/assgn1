MY MENU

IDLY

It is one of the most healthiest tiffin all the time. It is also called steamed rice cake. It has non-oil food. We can eat idly with Chutney, sambar etc., idly-sambar is famous combination in tamilnadu.

DOSA

Dosa is one of the most eaten tiffins in INDIA. It is good for health and best in taste. There are many types of dosas in india like masala dosa, paneer dosa etc.,

VADA

Vada is a category of savoury fried snacks from India. Different types of vadas can be described variously as fritters, cutlets, doughnuts, or dumplings.The various types of vadas are made from different ingredients, ranging from legumes to potatoes. They are often served as a breakfast item or a snack, and also used in other food preparations. Alternative names for this food include wada, vade, vadai, wadeh and bara.
